About Us

We are a student-led club at the Singapore Institute of Management (SIM), established in 2014. Our club caters to students who are passionate about data analytics and data science. We aim to provide a platform for both beginner and advanced learners to explore, learn, and excel in these fields. We do so by focusing on teaching data analytics and data science concepts.

Our Events & Collaborations

DACademy Track

A beginner-friendly curriculum track, featuring close to 20 hands-on coding lessons on developing basic data analytics skills such as: Sentiment Analysis, Web Scraping, EDA/Tableau, and SQL.

DSAcademy Track

An advanced curriculum track, featuring close to 20 hands-on coding lessons on developing high-level data science skills such as: Machine Learning, Generative AI, Deep Learning, and Data Engineering.

Our Timeline

August to September - Recruitment

During this period we are open to take on and conduct interviews for a new batch of subcommitee members for both our DAC and DSA tracks.

October to December - Curriculum

For these three months, we will teach our curriculum for our tracks from start to finish to build the skills of our subcomittee members.

December - Internal Projects

Subcommitee members are placed into groups and assigned to solve a prompt using the data analytics and data science skills that they have been taught.

They are then asked to present their final solution and code to be marked on their quality of implementation.

January to March - External Projects

We form partnerships with external companies, and distinguished subcomittee members use their skills to work on a project given by these companies usually over a one-to-three month timespan.

March - Annual General Meeting

We wrap up our operations for the current academic term and introduce a new batch of Executive Committee members to take over for the future.
